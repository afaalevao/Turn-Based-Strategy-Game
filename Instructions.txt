Instructions:

Screen Resolution adjustment:
	"+" to enlarge screen
	"-" to shrink screen

To begin:
	left click on player tanks to display available paths

	right click on highlighted square to move (can only move three spaces per tank per turn)

	if in range of enemy tank (5 spaces):
		 left click on player tank again and enemy tank will highlight blue
		right click on enemy tank to fire

	once turn is over (can't move anymore) press "R" to begin Computer move
	
	After computer move is over, player can play again by clicking on player tanks
	
	BUG: if player is in range of enemy, enemy will fire but the missiles will come out of the player tank instead of enemy tank
